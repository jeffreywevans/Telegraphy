# **Code of Conduct**

## **01**

*Judge contributions on merit*

We judge contributions on the quality of the work. We do not weigh personal identity, politics, background, creed, or biography. They are irrelevant here.

## **02**

*Conduct yourself accordingly*

Speak plainly. Disagree freely. Be respectful enough to remain useful.

## **03**

*Keep the standard brutally short*

Nothing else matters.

## **04**

*How to Contact*

Contributors wishing to raise private concerns about violations of these standards may perform interpretive dance until called upon.
